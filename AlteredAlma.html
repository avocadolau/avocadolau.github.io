Altered Alma

2D Environment Artist

Jun 2023 - Jun 2025

Altered Alma is a neon-filled 2D Metroidvania with the rhythm of cyberpunk RPG adventures! Where fast-paced combat meets strategic gameplay. Set against the backdrop of an alien-infused Neo Barcelona, "Altered Alma" blends the atmospheric tension of Blade Runner with the intricate spaceship and crew management of Mass Effect 2. This game is being developed with Unity Engine following an agile methodology

As a Metroidvania the game requires a huge amount of different rooms ambiented in different biomes. I was in charge to composite almost all rooms in the game, and illuminate them. Moreover, to achieve charming and memorable environments I also produced many VFX and shaders
