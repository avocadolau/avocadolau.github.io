Guardians of the Galaxy

Lead Artist

Feb 2023 - May 2023

Every year, students from CITM develop a videogame with an own game engine. This time, as Pixel Playground, we have developed a rogelike top-down shooter with Guardians of the Galaxy as theme and it was developed in the short period of time of 3 months.

As Lead Artist I was in charge of getting a unified low poly comic style by managing and organazing our art department.

My other role in this project was Character Artist. Modeling and rigging all characters. Due to the short amount of time I also performed some tasks like texturing environments, animating and UI design.
